### Our Presenter

[Tom Augspurger](https://twitter.com/tomaugspurger)

#### The Topic

We'll cover parallel programming in Python at a high level. We'll discuss the GIL and how it affects the choice between Multiprocessing and threaded parallelism. We'll briefly see the concurrent.futures interface, before going into more detail on dask and distributed.
